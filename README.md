# British Birdex 🐦

A Pokémon-style British bird spotting app for young birdwatchers.
Collect all 30 birds across 6 habitat types!

## Files
- `index.html` — the main app
- `birds.js` — all 30 bird data (facts, categories, stats)
- `illustrations.js` — SVG cartoon illustrations for every bird
- `manifest.json` — makes it installable on iPhone

---

## How to deploy (step by step)

### Step 1 — Create a GitHub account
Go to https://github.com and sign up if you don't have an account.

### Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** button in the top right and choose **New repository**
2. Name it `birdex` (or anything you like)
3. Set it to **Public**
4. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 — Upload the files
1. On your new repo page, click **Add file → Upload files**
2. Drag and drop all four files:
   - `index.html`
   - `birds.js`
   - `illustrations.js`
   - `manifest.json`
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 — Turn on GitHub Pages
1. Go to your repo's **Settings** tab
2. Scroll down to **Pages** in the left sidebar
3. Under **Source**, choose **Deploy from a branch**
4. Set branch to **main** and folder to **/ (root)**
5. Click **Save**

### Step 5 — Get your link
After about 60 seconds, refresh the Settings → Pages page.
You'll see a green box with your URL, something like:
`https://yourusername.github.io/birdex/`

### Step 6 — Add to iPhone home screen
1. Open the URL in **Safari** on your iPhone
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

The app will appear on your home screen like a real app! 🎉

## How to play
- Tap any bird card to see full details
- Tap **⚡ Capture!** when you spot the bird in real life
- Your progress saves automatically on your phone
- Use the tabs to filter by habitat type
- Check **⭐ My Dex** to see your collection

## The 6 habitat types
| Type | Emoji | Birds |
|------|-------|-------|
| Garden | 🏡 | Robin, Blue Tit, House Sparrow, Blackbird, Starling |
| Woodland | 🌳 | Great Spotted Woodpecker, Jay, Nuthatch, Tawny Owl, Great Tit, Treecreeper |
| Farmland | 🌾 | Lapwing, Barn Owl, Skylark, Yellowhammer, Pheasant |
| Wetland | 💧 | Kingfisher, Grey Heron, Mallard, Moorhen, Reed Warbler, Little Egret |
| Coastal | 🌊 | Puffin, Gannet, Oystercatcher, Herring Gull, Turnstone |
| Moorland | 🌿 | Red Grouse, Golden Eagle, Curlew |

Good luck catching them all!
